Welcome to Adam Grant Music



Welcome to Adam Grant Music.

I am an acoustic singer songwriter from Northern Ireland. Take a look around the site and do not hesitate to contact me for bookings or any other information.

From the age of 15 to 22, I was in 2 bands that did rather well on the local music scene. Performances in England, North and South Ireland, 2 professional EP's, local support for professional touring bands, it was a great and rewarding experience in my budding musical life.

After a few dabbles with other musical outputs, getting married, and having a daughter, in October 2015 I decided to pick up my guitar and try my hand as a solo performer. Influenced by Bob Dylan, Newton Faulkner, Noel Gallagher, Ed Sheeran, Bruce Springsteen, Blink-182 to name but a few, and a general love of indie, rock, and folk, I create music that hopefully appeals to many audiences. Since then, I have steadily been playing gigs around Co. Down and beyond, playing anywhere people would listen to my songs. I released my debut solo EP titled 'The Fight' in August 2016, and it is available at adamgrantmusic.bandcamp.com

In addition to my solo material, I also have a full set of cover songs suitable for bars, clubs, afternoon weddings, private functions etc. I have a wide range of songs that is bound to suit your needs. Feel free to message me to discuss bookings.

Thank you for taking the time to read this, and hopefully I will see you at a show!

Adam
